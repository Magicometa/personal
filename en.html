About me

Since the year 2000, I have expanded my knowledge in 3D modeling and animation using tools such as 3D Studio Max, Blender, and Zbrush. Additionally, I have complemented my skills with the use of Substance Painter and Marmoset Toolbag.


My focus is on rendering info-architecture and low-polygon modeling for video game development in Unreal Engine and Unity.


I have also ventured into the field of web programming, using languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, with the Django framework, and I have gained experience in using Git on Github.

3D Design

As a character and environment modeler for info-architecture, I have considered it essential to have a clear understanding of the polygonal construction of models, whether in high or low polygon count. The evolution of software requires constant adaptation to new programs that improve productivity, without forgetting the foundations on which we previously worked.


Therefore, it is important to stay up-to-date on the latest trends and 3D modeling tools, while maintaining a solid understanding of the fundamentals of modeling.


Since I began my career in 2000, I have developed skills in animation, lighting, rendering, and VFX. I have gained experience working in different places, such as Movistar, the School of Art in Granada, and on personal projects.

VideoGames

My technical knowledge in video game development ranges from integrating 3D models into Unreal Engine or Unity engines, to implementing textures and animations in those models.


In my career, I have been good at collaborating with my colleagues, helping them improve their performance and skills, and learning from their progress. I have even had the opportunity to teach in this field.


With the aim of continuing to grow, I started studying programming to master the different development areas in UnrealEngine. Currently, I can add the ability to create shaders in UnrealEngine.

Programming

I have always been motivated by knowledge, and when I delved into the world of video game development, I became interested in programming. Therefore, to take my first steps, I chose to study at UOC, where I acquired knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Python.


I did all of this with the goal of having an introductory foundation in programming for UnrealEngine, where I have gained skills in creating shaders. Now, my objective is to continue expanding my knowledge in the complete configuration of assets and characters in Unreal.


Whether as a hobby or work, I have always enjoyed telling stories and interacting with them. This interest led me to delve into the world of multimedia environments, without neglecting other passions I have, such as video, photography, music, among others.
